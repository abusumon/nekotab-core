Debate Motions Database for BP, WSDC, PF, LD and Policy

NekoTab hosts a searchable debate motions database with over 36,000 real tournament motions. If you are preparing for British Parliamentary, World Schools, Public Forum, Lincoln-Douglas, Policy, or Asians/Australs rounds, this page helps you find relevant motion sets quickly.

Why this debate motions page is useful

  • Search by keyword to find motions on any major topic area
  • Filter by style, level, year, region and motion type
  • Use real tournament context, not synthetic examples
  • Open sources when tab links are available

Best way to use the motion bank

Start on the Global Motion Bank, choose your format, then narrow by topic and year. For preparation, collect 10 to 20 motions from similar tournament levels and build short case outlines for each side.
