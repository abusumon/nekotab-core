Where Your Money Goes

What your support funds

Every contribution goes directly into the four pillars that make NekoTab reliable, powerful, and free for the global debate community.

🖥️
Server Infrastructure

Production hosting, database costs, CDN, and storage — the backbone that keeps NekoTab fast and always online for tournaments around the world.

🤖
AI Motion Analysis

Our AI-powered motion analysis helps debaters prep smarter. API costs aren't free — your support keeps this feature available to everyone at no charge.

🚀
New Features

From real-time draws to digital ballots and standings — features your community requests take real time to build. Supporters make the roadmap move faster.

🔒
Security & Reliability

SSL certificates, DDoS protection, uptime monitoring, and regular security audits — essential costs that keep tournament data safe.
