Why Use NekoTab for Debate Registration?

Debate tournaments involve complex registration logistics: teams of 2–4 speakers, independent adjudicators, observers, and sometimes coaches and support staff. Collecting this data cleanly and managing it through to the tab system is where most tournament organizers lose hours.

NekoTab's integrated registration forms solve this by keeping everything in one place — from the registration form that participants fill in, to the submissions dashboard where the tab team reviews them, to the tournament tab where participants are assigned to draws.

What You Can Build

👥 Team Registration Forms

Collect team names, speaker names, institutions, email contacts, years of experience, dietary requirements, accommodation needs, and any tournament-specific fields. Supports BP (4-speaker teams), Australs (3-speaker teams), and other formats.

⚖️ Judge Registration Forms

Collect adjudicator names, institutions, clash declarations, availability by round, CA experience, wing judging preferences, panel chairing history, and payment details. All in a clean, shareable form URL.

📝 Custom Fields

Add any field type: short text, long text, multiple choice, checkboxes, dropdowns, date pickers, and file uploads. Build the exact form your tournament needs, not a one-size-fits-all template.

📊 Submissions Dashboard

All form submissions appear in your workspace dashboard in real-time. Review registrations, search by institution or name, mark entries as confirmed, and export data to CSV for use in the tab system.

How It Works

  1. Create a free workspace at nekotab.app
  2. Go to Forms in your workspace sidebar and click "Create Form"
  3. Add your fields — name, institution, team members, judge details, dietary needs, etc.
  4. Publish the form and share the public URL with participants
  5. Review submissions in your workspace dashboard as they come in
  6. Export or import registrant data into the tournament tab system

FAQ: Debate Registration Forms

Is the forms builder free?
Yes. The NekoTab forms builder is included free in every workspace. Create unlimited forms and collect unlimited submissions at no cost.
What types of forms can I create?
Team registration, individual speaker registration, judge registration, observer forms, workshop sign-ups, participant surveys, feedback forms, and any other custom form your tournament needs.
Can I export registrations?
Yes. Submissions are viewable in your workspace dashboard and can be exported to CSV for use in the tab system or your own spreadsheets.
Do participants need a NekoTab account to fill in a form?
No. Public registration forms can be filled in without a NekoTab account. Participants just open the form URL and submit — no sign-in required.
