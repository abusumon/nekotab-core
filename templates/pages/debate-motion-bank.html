What Is the NekoTab Debate Motion Bank?

The NekoTab Motion Bank is a continuously updated database of competitive debate motions collected from tournaments worldwide. Whether you compete in British Parliamentary, World Schools (WSDC), Asian Parliamentary, Public Forum, Lincoln-Douglas, or Policy debate, you'll find motions from your circuit — organized, filtered, and ready to use for preparation, coaching, and research.

Unlike static PDFs or spreadsheets, the NekoTab Motion Bank is a fully interactive search engine. Filter by any combination of format, year, region, topic area, motion type, and difficulty to find exactly what you need in seconds.

Debate Formats Covered

  • 🏛 British Parliamentary (BP) — THW, THBT, THP, THS, THO, THR motions from university and school circuits including EUDC, WUDC, Australs, Asian BP, IONA, and hundreds of national opens.
  • 🌏 WSDC / World Schools — Prepared and impromptu motions from the World Schools Debating Championships and national school circuits.
  • 🗣 Asian Parliamentary — Motions from Asian Debating Championships and regional BP circuits across South and Southeast Asia.
  • 📋 Public Forum — Resolved: topics from NSDA-sanctioned US high school competitions by semester and year.
  • ⚖️ Lincoln-Douglas — Bimonthly LD topics focused on ethics, values, and philosophy from US high school competition.
  • 📑 Policy Debate — Annual policy resolutions from US high school and college cross-examination debate.

Key Features of the Motion Bank

🔍 Powerful Search & Filtering

Search by keyword, tournament name, or motion text. Filter by format, year range, region, topic area (Politics, Economics, Law, Environment, Social Justice, and 20+ more), motion type (THW/THBT/THP/THS/THO/THR), level, and difficulty.

🤖 AI-Powered Motion Analysis

Every motion page includes AI-generated difficulty scores (technical, complexity, abstraction, depth), burden analysis showing Gov/Prop vs Opp advantage, and hidden assumptions — helping coaches and debaters assess motion balance at a glance.

📝 Community Case Outlines

Each motion page hosts community-contributed case outlines for Government/Proposition and Opposition sides. Vote on the best outlines to surface the strongest arguments, and use them as starting points for your own case preparation.

📊 Motions by Year, Tournament & Region

Browse dedicated year-by-year collections (2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025) and tournament-specific collections (EUDC, WUDC, Australs, WSDC, Asian BP, IONA, USUDC, and more).

FAQ: Debate Motion Bank

What is a debate motion bank?
A debate motion bank is a searchable database of competitive debate motions (topics/resolutions) from real tournaments. NekoTab's Motion Bank lets you filter thousands of BP, WSDC, PF, LD, Policy, and AP motions from international competitions worldwide.
Is the NekoTab Motion Bank free?
Yes, completely free. You can search, filter, and analyse debate motions without creating an account. No subscription, no paywall — all data is freely accessible at nekotab.app/motions/.
Does NekoTab have AI motion analysis?
Yes. Each individual motion page includes AI-powered difficulty ratings (technical, complexity, abstraction, depth) and burden scores showing which side has the stronger structural advantage. Open any motion from the bank to see its analysis.
Can I find EUDC, WUDC, Australs, and WSDC motions?
Yes. The Motion Bank includes motion sets from EUDC, WUDC, Australs, WSDC, WJDC, Asian BP Championships, and hundreds of other national and open competitions. Search by tournament name to pull the full motion set from any specific event.
Can I submit a motion to the NekoTab Motion Bank?
Yes. Visit nekotab.app/motions-bank/submit/ to submit a motion. Submissions are reviewed before being added to the public database.
