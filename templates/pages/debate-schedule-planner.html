What Is NekoTab's Debate Schedule Planner?

The NekoTab Schedule Planner is a purpose-built tool for debate tournament organizers. Create a structured, shareable schedule for your tournament that covers every session — debate rounds, breaks, social events, meals, and ceremonies — all in one clean timeline that participants can access on any device.

Whether you're running a small society tournament or a large international competition, the schedule planner helps you communicate the programme clearly and update it in real-time as the event unfolds.

Schedule Planner Features

📅 Multi-Day Schedule Builder

Add schedule entries across multiple days. Organize sessions by date, set start and end times, and add descriptions for each item in the programme — from Day 1 registration to Day 3 finals.

🌐 Public Schedule Page

Every NekoTab schedule automatically generates a public, shareable page. Participants can view the tournament programme without logging in — just share the URL and they're set.

⚡ Live Updates

Update the schedule in real-time during the tournament. If a round is delayed or a session time changes, update it in your workspace and participants immediately see the latest version.

🗂 All Session Types

Plan any type of session: preliminary rounds, break announcements, elimination rounds, keynote speeches, social events, meals, transport, accommodation check-in, and awards ceremonies.

Why Debate Tournaments Need a Schedule Planner

Poor schedule communication is one of the most common sources of confusion at debate tournaments. Participants arrive late, miss rounds, or don't know where to be. A clear, accessible schedule solves this:

  • Reduces "what's next?" questions to the tab team and organizers
  • Keeps participants informed across different time zones and venues
  • Helps judges plan travel by showing session start and end times
  • Documents the full programme for participants to share and reference
  • Works on mobile — participants check it on their phones between rounds
