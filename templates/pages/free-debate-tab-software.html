Why NekoTab is Free

NekoTab is built on Tabbycat, the open-source debate tabulation engine used by hundreds of tournaments worldwide. NekoTab hosts it for you — no server setup, no Docker, no command line — so tournament directors can focus on running their tournament rather than managing infrastructure.

Debate Formats Supported

  • British Parliamentary (BP) — four-team draws, side balancing, iron person handling
  • World Schools (WSDC) — three-member teams, WSDC-style speaker scoring
  • Australs / 3v3 — three-team parliamentary, Australs power-pairing rules
  • Asian Parliamentary (AP) — three-member team format
  • Public Forum (PF) — two-person team format
  • Lincoln-Douglas (LD) — one-on-one individual format
  • Policy Debate — two-person team, cross-examination format
  • Congress Debate — legislative format with speaker scoring

Key Tabulation Features

🔀 Automated Draws

Power-pairing, side balancing, pull-up handling, and room allocation — all automated. Override any pairing manually with drag-and-drop controls.

⚖️ Adjudicator Allocation

Smart adjudicator assignment with automatic conflict detection (institution, personal, history). Set clash priorities and ranking scores per judge.

📱 Digital Ballots

Adjudicators submit scores from their phones. NekoTab validates entries in real time and prevents ballot confirmation until all required fields are complete.

📊 Live Public Results

Participants and coaches can follow live standings, speaker scores, and break announcements on the public results page — accessible from any device without logging in.

🏆 Break Calculations

Automatic break calculations for any bracket size, with tie-breaking rules configurable per tournament. Publish team and speaker breaks when you're ready.

📤 Data Export

Export draws, results, and speaker sheets to spreadsheet format. Import participant lists from CSV or Google Sheets to save data entry time at registration.

FAQ: Free Debate Tab Software

Is NekoTab really free?
Yes. No per-tournament fees, no subscription, no setup costs. NekoTab is free for all debate tournament directors.
What formats does NekoTab support?
BP, WSDC, Australs, AP, PF, LD, Policy, and Congress. All formats are configurable for speaker counts, sides, and scoring rules.
How does NekoTab compare to Tabroom.com?
NekoTab is stronger for parliamentary formats (BP, WSDC, Australs) and includes a motion bank, ticketing, website builder, and schedule planner that Tabroom.com doesn't have. Tabroom.com has stronger support for US-based formats at the school circuit level.
Can NekoTab handle large tournaments?
Yes. NekoTab is used by national and international championships with 100+ teams and hundreds of adjudicators.
