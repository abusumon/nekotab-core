Sell Tickets for Any Debate Event

NekoTab's ticketing system is built for the kinds of events debate communities run: university society tournaments, open circuit competitions, workshops, training camps, charity debates, and social events. Whether you're expecting 30 participants or 300, NekoTab handles registration and ticketing at any scale.

Ticketing Features

🎟 Multiple Ticket Tiers

Create multiple ticket tiers for the same event — Speaker, Judge, Observer, VIP, Early Bird, and more. Set individual prices, descriptions, and quantity limits for each tier.

🆓 Free & Paid Tickets

Offer free registration for society members or free-entry events alongside paid tiers for guests and external participants — all in a single event setup.

📅 Event Management

Add event details: name, date, venue, cover image, and description. Each event gets a public page at nekotab.app/tickets/yourorg/event-name/ that you can share with participants.

✅ Attendee Check-In

Manage your attendee list in the workspace dashboard and use the check-in tool at the event to verify ticket holders quickly at the registration desk.

🌐 Public Event Listing

Your events appear in the NekoTab global events directory at nekotab.app/tickets/ — giving your tournament visibility to the broader debate community beyond your own social media reach.

📊 Registration Tracking

See real-time registration counts, remaining ticket quantities per tier, and total revenue from your workspace dashboard. Know exactly how many participants have registered before the event starts.

How to Start Selling Tickets for Your Debate Event

  1. Create a free workspace at nekotab.app
  2. Go to Events in your workspace sidebar and click "Create Event"
  3. Add event details — name, date, venue, cover image, description
  4. Add ticket tiers — set prices, quantities, and descriptions for each tier
  5. Publish the event — your event page goes live immediately
  6. Share the event URL with participants via social media, email, or WhatsApp
  7. Manage attendees from your workspace dashboard on event day

FAQ: Debate Event Ticketing

Can I sell tickets for debate tournaments on NekoTab?
Yes. Create an event in your workspace, add ticket tiers with prices and quantities, and share the public event URL for participants to purchase tickets or register.
Is it free to use NekoTab ticketing?
The NekoTab ticketing platform is free for debate organizations. You can create events and manage registrations without a platform fee from NekoTab.
Can I offer free tickets alongside paid ones?
Yes. NekoTab supports both free and paid ticket tiers within the same event — useful for giving free access to society members while charging external participants.
Where do participants find debate events on NekoTab?
Participants can browse all events at nekotab.app/tickets/ or go directly to your organization's event page. You can also share the event URL directly.
