How BP Draws Work in NekoTab

NekoTab's draw engine automatically generates fair BP rooms by power-pairing teams based on current standings. Side balancing ensures no team is unfairly placed, and conflict detection prevents institutional clashes. Pull-ups are handled automatically when bracket sizes are uneven.
