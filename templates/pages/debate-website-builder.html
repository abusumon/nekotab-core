Why Debate Organizations Need a Website

A professional website is the foundation of your debate organization's online presence. It tells new members where to find you, shows your tournament history and achievements, lists upcoming events, and builds credibility with sponsors and partner institutions.

Without a website, debate organizations rely on social media alone — which limits discoverability, makes it hard to share structured information, and means losing everything if a platform changes its algorithm. A NekoTab website gives your organization a permanent, searchable home on the web.
