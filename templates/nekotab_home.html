FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Which formats does NekoTab support?

British Parliamentary (BP), Australs/3v3, WSDC-style, and other parliamentary formats. You can configure speaker counts, sides, and scoring to match your needs.

Can I import teams, judges, and venues?

Yes — CSV imports are fully supported. You can also manage availability, conflicts, and institutional clashes from the admin panel.

Is it free to use?

Yes — NekoTab is completely free to use. No per-tournament fees, no hidden charges. Create an account and start tabbing immediately. You can also self-host for full data sovereignty.

How do I create a tournament?

Just sign up, click "Create Tournament", fill in a name and number of rounds, and you're ready to go. No approval or verification needed.

Can participants submit their own ballots?

Yes — you can enable public ballot submission or use private URLs sent to participants for secure, individual access.

How does the Motion Bank work?

The Motion Bank is a searchable database of thousands of real debate motions from tournaments worldwide. You can filter by format (BP, Australs, WSDC), region, year, theme, and difficulty level. Each motion includes metadata and community ratings.

Can I self-host NekoTab?

Yes. NekoTab is fully open-source and can be deployed on your own infrastructure using Docker. Self-hosting gives you complete data sovereignty and customization options.
