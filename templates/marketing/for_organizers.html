How the Workspace Works

From sign-up to running your first tournament in 5 steps.

1

Create Your Workspace

Pick a name and slug for your organization. You get your own subdomain instantly.

2

Invite Your Team

Add tabmasters, editors, and viewers. Everyone gets role-based access to the tournaments they need.

3

Create Tournaments

Launch tournaments from your dashboard. They live under your organization with shared settings and members.

4

Run & Manage

Generate draws, collect ballots, release results — all the tabulation tools you know, now under one roof.

5

Archive & Grow

Past tournaments stay in your archive. Build a track record and grow your circuit over multiple seasons.
